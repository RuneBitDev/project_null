------------------------------
CYB3R GW3NT: Official Rulebook
------------------------------
Cyberpunk 2077 version of Gwent Card Game
- Goal: Have the highest Total Strength on the board after both players Pass
- The Catch: Your Strength only matters if your units survive the Firefight
---------------------------------------------------------------------	

Game Progression
	- Currency: eddies
	- Gamemodes: 
		Campaign to unlock new factions
		Free Play (different difficulties) to earn eddies
		Sandbox to play around
	- Card shop:
		packs contain cards, weapons, cyberware or armor
		packs cost eddies		


The Deck Builder:
You don't just find cards; you "chrome" them
	- add and remove cards (must be in the same faction or neutral)
	- Ripperdoc: modify cyberware and gear of card
	- Workshop: dismantle (gain materials) and upgrade gear & cyberware
	
	
The Unit Anatomy
three primary stats, predefined gear and cyberware slots:
	- Strength (S): Their contribution to the win total
	- Armor (A): The "gate" that prevents destruction
	- Attack (Atk): How much Armor they strip from an enemy per turn
	- Mod Slot: A slot for a "Weapon" or "Cyberware" card
other stats: 
	- rarity, slots (amount of slotable cyberware)
	
	
The 4-Row Dynamic
	- Melee: can only attack melee row
	- Ranged: can attack melee row (and further with mods)
	- Heavy: can attack any row, but attacks every other turn
	- Net: no attack, quickhacks (status effects, buffs, debuffs)
		
		
		
---------------------------------------------------------------------		
The Cyber-Gwent Combat Loop:
1. Round Start
	- Draw: draw cards
	- Maintenance: any "Start of Round" status effects trigger
2. The Play Phase (P1 then P2)
	Players take turns performing one action, must choose one:
	- Deploy: unit card -> 4 rows (Melee, Ranged, Heavy, Net) + choose stance (aggressive, supressive, defensive)
	- Execute: special card
	- Pass: no more cards can be played
	+ additionally stances can be changed
3. The Resolution Phase (The "Firefight")
	- Calculate Damage: Units compare their Attack value (plus Weapon Mods) against the target's Armor
	- Break Thresholds: if Damage > Armor -> graveyard
	- Armor Reduction: if Damage ≤ Armor -> reduce armor by attack amount
	- Net Actions: Quickhacks resolve here
4. End of Turn
	- Check State: if both passed -> 5. else -> 2.
5. End of Round / Game
	- The "Chrome Graveyard": all units on the board are cleared
	- Self-Destruct/Persistence: certain cards might stay on board or return to hand
	- Winner: 2 rounds won
